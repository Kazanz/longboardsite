FAQ

What if i've never ridden a longboard before?

We've tested our board out with many first time riders. Because you are no longer focusing on pumping and recentering your weight, Many first time riders find that they learn much quicker with the Vitesse board.

How do I control the board?

A Bluetooth wireless remote controls our board acceleration. The control allows you to speed up, slow down, brake, and switch direction. You turn by shifting your body weight as if on a regular long board.

How does it charge?

The board charges using a DC power supply, and can take up to two hours for a full charge. The remote is charged using a micro USB and will take 1 hour for a full charge .

Can the board go up hills?

Yes! both our single motor and our dual motor boards can ride up small to medium inclines. Depending on your weight,the board will go faster or slower. For a faster up hill ride, it is best to approach the hill with some momentum.

Can I ride my board on wet pavement?

Because the motor is open to the elements, we suggest that it not be ridden through puddles or in the rain. Riding your board on wet pavement is okay.

What is the max rider weight?

The maximum rider weight is 260lbs.
